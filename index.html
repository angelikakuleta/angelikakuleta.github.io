About me

Hi. I'm just a girl who is fascinated by technical sciences. I come from a small town in the Lubuskie Province, where time has stopped in the 90s. My interests are focused on software engineering, analytics and machine learning (the last one only in theory for now). I have commercial experience in administering the TETA HR system application, creating reports using SQL language and configuring the workflow mechanisms of the mensioned application. I am an enthusiast of teamwork and clean code. In my spare time I study cognitive science, trying to discover how my brain works and day by day delving into transhumanist idelogies with a hint of skepticism. Kyokushin karate training has become my second passion, which allows me to keep a balance in my life. I am focused on personal development and gaining experience on many levels.
